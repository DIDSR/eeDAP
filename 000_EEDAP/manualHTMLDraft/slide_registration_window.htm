Slide Registration Window

When the MicroRT mode is selected the Slide Registration window will open. The microscope camera will also engage, and a video preview window will appear. In this step the Administrator verifies and registers the glass slides to the corresponding WSIs. This ensures that the reader’s FOV in MicroRT will be the same as the ROI presented in the Digital mode. Refer to the “MicroRT Registration” section for details.
