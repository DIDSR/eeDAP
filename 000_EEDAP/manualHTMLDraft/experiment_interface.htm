Experiment Interface

Once you input the reader's ID, the evaluation tasks will begin when the ROI Image Presentation Screen opens. It is presented in a fashion identical to the Digital mode experiment. The Administrator is expected to read the tasks to the reader and record the responses of the Reader so that the Reader may stay engaged with the microscope. Please refer to the section on “Tasks Instructions”.
