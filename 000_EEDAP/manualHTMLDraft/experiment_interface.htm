Experiment Interface

Once theinput reader ID, the evaluation tasks begin when the ROI Image Presentation Screen opens. It is presented in a fashion identical to the Digital mode experiment with the addition of the Video Preview window. The Administrator is expected to read the tasks to the reader and record the responses of the Reader so that the Reader may stay engaged with the microscope. Please refer to the section on “Tasks”. The Administrator is also expected to monitor the Video Preview to confirm proper registration throughout the experiment.
