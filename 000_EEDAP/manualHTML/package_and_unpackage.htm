Package and Unpackage

Package:

  • Camera
  • Unplug USB3 cable from camera.
  • Use allen wrench to take off camera (adapter) and install caps.
  • Separate camera adapter(if necessary) and the camera, and put the cap on camera, adapter and microscope.
  • Put camera and adapter in pink bubble bag and put the bag in microscope frame box (on the top of the paper frame).
  • Stage
  • Unplug stage cable from stage and controller.
  • Unplug power, joystick cables from stage controller.
  • Put all cables and joystick in cable bag (bag from prior box).
  • Use allen wrench to take off stage from Microscope.
  • Put stage in stage box.
  • Use bubble paper to package stage controller and stage. Then put them in the main microscope box.
  • Microscope
  • Unplug the power cable and put in cable bag.
    2. Use allen wrench to take off the eyepiece part, and put eyepiece into eyepiece box.
  • Put the microscope in microscope frame box (tape over hole where eyepiece attaches).
  • Put eyepiece, frame boxes in main microscope box.
  • Put microscope cover in main box.
  • Computer
  • Unplug power, display and camera USB cable.
  • Put cables, computer, mouse and working dock in computer bag.
  • Screen
  • Unplug display cable and put in screen box.
  • Put screen in box.
  • Other
  • Allen wrench bag (put in main box).
  • Power strip (put in screen box).
  • Microscope cover (put in main box).
 
 

Un-package:

Bring all small boxes from microscope main box.
  • Microscope and camera
  • Bring camera and adapter from frame box.
  • Bring microscope frame out from box and remove the cover.
  • Bring microscope eyepiece out from box.
  • Use Allen wrench install eyepiece, adapter (remove caps).
  • Install camera on adapter.
  • Plug in the power.
  • Stage
  • Bring stage and controller out.
  • Install stage to microscope by Allen wrench.
  • Connect controller with stage, joystick and computer.
  • Plug in the power.
  • Screen
  • Connect screen with computer and plug in the power.
  • Computer
  • Plug computer on working dock.
  • Plug in camera, controller and screen.
